About Us

OVRLOOX isn’t just a fashion label — it’s a movement. We are here to redefine men’s fashion by bringing the community into the heart of creation. Founded by Mohammed Sohail and co-founded by K. Sri Mallik, OVRLOOX is built on a bold idea: you vote, we create. The most demanded designs are the ones we drop — ensuring that every collection reflects the style and voice of our inner circle. With limited drops, affordable pricing, and uncompromised quality, we make sure fashion stays exclusive yet accessible. We believe clothing should be more than fabric — it’s a statement of identity, confidence, and belonging. When you wear OVRLOOX, you’re not just wearing clothes, you’re wearing the choice of a community.
